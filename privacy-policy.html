The Greater Hartford Threshold Choir ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thresholdchoirhartford.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when visiting or using our site. By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

Important: The only way we collect personal information on this website is through the Request a Sing form. We do not collect personal data through any other page or feature of this site.

01Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit the Request a Sing form on our website. This form is the sole mechanism through which we gather personal data. We do not collect personal information through any other page, feature, or interaction on this site.

Through the Request a Sing form, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Your name and email address so we can follow up with you about your request.
  • Location Information: The city or general area where the sing is being requested, used solely to coordinate our volunteer singers for the visit. We do not collect precise GPS data.
  • Request Details: Information about the person you are requesting a sing for, such as their situation, availability, and any preferences you share to help us serve them with care.

We also automatically collect limited technical data when you visit our site (such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited) for site maintenance and analytics purposes only — not tied to your identity.

02How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the Request a Sing form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your request and coordinate a bedside singing visit
  • Communicate with you about scheduling, timing, and logistics
  • Match your request with available volunteer singers in your area
  • Follow up with you after the visit if appropriate
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We will never use your information for purposes unrelated to fulfilling your Request a Sing submission or the Greater Hartford Threshold Choir's mission, without your explicit consent.

03Location Data

Location information is collected only through the Request a Sing form, where you provide the city or general area where a singing visit is needed. This information is used solely to coordinate and dispatch available volunteer singers near that location.

We do not collect precise GPS coordinates, and we do not share location data with third parties for advertising purposes. Location is used only to fulfill your specific singing request.

You are never required to enable device location access to use this website.

04How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate our website (e.g., email services, hosting), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our organization or others.
  • Organizational Affiliates: We may share information with the broader Threshold Choir network (thresholdchoir.org) when relevant to connecting you with services.

05Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Email addresses used for our mailing list will be removed promptly upon your request to unsubscribe.

06Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site. These may include:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand aggregate site usage (e.g., Google Analytics)

You may configure your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

07Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Withdraw consent for location data collection

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

08Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

09Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
